Assumptions

  • Before crossover, equivalent shock spring rate is \(k_{eq}=\frac{k_t k_m}{k_t + k_m}\).
  • After crossover, shock spring rate is the main spring rate \(k_m\).
  • Wheel rate is shock rate multiplied by motion ratio squared.

Engineering note: this is a simple quasi-static model for visualization. Real coilover behavior can differ because of spring free lengths, coil bind, divider engagement, dual-rate hardware stack height, motion-ratio change through travel, and bump-stop contribution.
